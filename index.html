Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. First-time visitors can get a one-day free pass. Just bring an ID and come during working hours.

What do I need to bring?

Workout clothes, shoes, and a towel. We provide lockers. You can buy water and snacks at the counter.

Can I freeze my membership?

For monthly plans, no. For 3-month and yearly plans, you can freeze once for up to 2 weeks with prior notice.

Is there parking?

Yes. We have free parking for members right outside the building.
